Coaching Philosophy

With extensive experience as both a national-level player and international coach, V Jyothi Swarupa brings a unique perspective to badminton coaching. Her journey from being India No. 7 in Women's Doubles to coaching the Indian National Team demonstrates her deep understanding of the sport at the highest levels. She is committed to nurturing young talent and developing players who can compete internationally, including representing India professionally.
